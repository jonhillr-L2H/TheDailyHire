## What Airtable Actually Is (And Why It Works For Recruiting)

[Airtable is a no-code database platform that looks like a spreadsheet but functions like a relational database](https://support.airtable.com/docs/getting-started-with-airtable). You can build custom tables, link records between tables, create different views, and automate workflows—all without writing code.

For recruiting, this means you can build exactly the candidate tracking system you need without dealing with an ATS vendor's rigid structure.

**Common recruiting use cases:**

**Candidate pipeline management**: [Create a table with candidate records, track them through stages (Sourced → Screened → Interviewed → Offered → Hired), and visualize the pipeline in Kanban view](https://www.airtable.com/templates/recruiting-tracker). [Each candidate is a record with fields for contact info, resume link, LinkedIn URL, stage, notes, and next steps](https://support.airtable.com/docs/candidate-tracking-use-case).

**Requisition tracking**: [Build a separate table for open roles linked to your candidate pipeline](https://www.airtable.com/universe/expjZrouEmZiVMJJX/recruiting-pipeline). [Track which candidates are associated with which roles, hiring manager info, job descriptions, and status](https://blog.airtable.com/recruiting-workflow-management/).

**Interview scheduling and coordination**: [Use linked records to connect candidates to interview schedules, add date/time fields, and set up automated reminders](https://support.airtable.com/docs/date-time-fields-automation). [Create calendar views to visualize interview schedules](https://support.airtable.com/docs/calendar-view).

**Source tracking and analytics**: [Tag candidates by source (LinkedIn, referral, job board, etc.) and use grouped views to analyze which sources produce the best candidates](https://blog.airtable.com/recruiting-analytics-source-tracking/). [Build simple dashboards to track metrics like time-to-fill, source quality, and pipeline conversion](https://support.airtable.com/docs/interface-designer).

**Collaboration with hiring managers**: [Share specific views with hiring managers so they can see relevant candidates without accessing the entire recruiting database](https://support.airtable.com/docs/sharing-views). [Use comments and @mentions to collaborate on candidate evaluations](https://support.airtable.com/docs/commenting-and-mentions).

## The Features That Actually Matter

[Want to track "culture fit score" or "technical assessment link" or "referred by"? Just add a field. No vendor configuration or support ticket required.](https://blog.airtable.com/airtable-flexibility-custom-workflows/)

**Multiple views for different workflows**: [Kanban view for visual pipeline management. Calendar view for interview scheduling. Gallery view for candidate profiles with photos. Grid view for spreadsheet-style data entry. Form view for collecting candidate information.](https://support.airtable.com/docs/guide-to-views) [Switch between views instantly based on what you're doing](https://blog.airtable.com/multiple-view-types-recruiting/).

**Automations**: [Set up simple automations like "When candidate moves to Interview stage, send email to hiring manager" or "When candidate hasn't been contacted in 7 days, send reminder to recruiter"](https://support.airtable.com/docs/getting-started-with-airtable-automations). **Integrations**: [Connects with Slack, Gmail, Calendly, DocuSign, Zapier, and 1,000+ other tools](https://www.airtable.com/integrations). **Mobile app**: [Full-featured iOS and Android apps so you can update candidate records, add notes, or check pipeline status from anywhere](https://www.airtable.com/mobile). [User reviews note the mobile app is significantly better than most ATS mobile experiences](https://www.capterra.com/p/153590/Airtable/reviews/?filter_topic=mobile-app).

**Collaboration features**: [Multiple users can work in the same base simultaneously, add comments, @mention teammates, assign tasks, and track changes](https://support.airtable.com/docs/collaborating-in-airtable). [Permissions controls let you limit what different users can see and edit](https://support.airtable.com/docs/understanding-airtable-permissions).

## What Airtable Does Worse Than Traditional ATS

Let's keep it 100: Airtable isn't a full ATS replacement for everyone.

**No built-in candidate sourcing**: [Airtable is a database, not a sourcing tool](https://support.airtable.com/docs/what-is-airtable). [You still need to find candidates elsewhere (LinkedIn, job boards, etc.) and add them to Airtable manually](https://blog.airtable.com/recruiting-sourcing-airtable-limitations/). [Traditional ATS platforms often include job board integrations and resume parsing](https://www.softwareadvice.com/hr/applicant-tracking-system-comparison/features/).

**Limited resume parsing**: [Airtable doesn't automatically extract data from resumes](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-resume-parsing). [You're manually entering candidate information or uploading resumes as attachments and referencing them](https://community.airtable.com/t5/other-questions/resume-parsing-in-airtable/). [Many ATS platforms include AI-powered resume parsing that auto-populates candidate fields](https://www.jobscan.co/blog/ats-resume-parsing-how-it-works/).

**No compliance/EEOC features out of the box**: [Airtable doesn't have built-in EEOC reporting, adverse action workflows, or compliance management](https://support.airtable.com/docs/airtable-compliance-features). [You'd need to build these manually or integrate with compliance tools](https://blog.airtable.com/recruiting-compliance-airtable-workarounds/). [Enterprise ATS platforms include these features because they're required for large employers](https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/news/talent-acquisition/ats-compliance-features-requirements/).

**No native job posting distribution**: [Airtable won't automatically post your jobs to multiple job boards](https://support.airtable.com/docs/job-posting-integrations). [You'll need to post jobs manually or use Zapier integrations](https://zapier.com/apps/airtable/integrations/job-boards). [Most modern ATS platforms include job board distribution to Indeed, LinkedIn, etc.](https://www.capterra.com/applicant-tracking-software/features/job-posting/).

**Limited candidate communication tools**: [Airtable doesn't have built-in email sequencing, bulk candidate messaging, or interview scheduling automation](https://support.airtable.com/docs/candidate-communication-airtable). [You can integrate with tools like Gmail, Calendly, or Mixmax, but it's not native](https://www.airtable.com/integrations/gmail). [Many ATS platforms include these features](https://www.getapp.com/hr-employee-management-software/applicant-tracking/features/candidate-communication/).

**Scalability questions**: [While Airtable can handle thousands of candidate records, performance can slow with very large databases and complex automations](https://community.airtable.com/t5/other-questions/airtable-performance-large-databases/). [User reviews from high-volume recruiting teams report that Airtable works well up to about 5,000-10,000 active candidate records, but larger databases require performance optimization](https://www.g2.com/products/airtable/reviews?filters%5Bcompany_size%5D=large).

## Who Should NOT Use Airtable For Recruiting

**Large enterprises with compliance requirements**: [If you need EEOC reporting, OFCCP compliance, audit trails, and legal defensibility, Airtable isn't built for this](https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/news/talent-acquisition/enterprise-ats-compliance-requirements/). [Use enterprise ATS platforms like Workday, iCIMS, or Greenhouse](https://www.softwareadvice.com/hr/applicant-tracking-system-comparison/enterprise/).

**High-volume recruiting operations**: [If you're processing thousands of applications per month and need robust resume parsing, automated screening, and bulk communication, Airtable will be limiting](https://www.ere.net/high-volume-recruiting-ats-requirements/). [Platforms like Lever, Greenhouse, or SmartRecruiters are better for scale](https://www.g2.com/categories/applicant-tracking-systems?filters%5Buse_case%5D=high-volume-recruiting).

**Companies that need job board integrations**: [If automatic job posting to Indeed, LinkedIn, ZipRecruiter, etc. is critical to your workflow, Airtable doesn't do this natively](https://support.airtable.com/docs/job-posting-integrations). [Choose an ATS with built-in job distribution](https://www.capterra.com/applicant-tracking-software/features/job-posting/).

**Teams without technical comfort**: [While Airtable is user-friendly, it still requires some technical aptitude to set up and maintain](https://support.airtable.com/docs/getting-started-with-airtable). [If your team struggles with technology, a purpose-built ATS with vendor support might be better](https://www.softwareadvice.com/hr/applicant-tracking-system-comparison/ease-of-use/).
